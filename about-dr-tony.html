About

The vet who believes healing happens at home.

"When pets feel safe, we can focus on what matters — understanding them, not rushing them."

Tony started Tony's Mobile Vets after years in busy clinic waiting rooms, watching nervous pets and worried owners try to squeeze real care into a fifteen-minute slot. He knew there had to be a kinder way — one that gave families time, space, and honest advice in the place their pets love most: home.

Meet Dr. Tony.

Tony is a UK-qualified veterinary surgeon (RCVS registered) with years of experience caring for dogs, cats, and small animals across London. He is known for his calm manner, curiosity, and clear explanations — the vet who always finds time to answer one more question.

Why Tony built TMV

From rushed visits to favourite appointments.

Tony created Tony's Mobile Vets around a simple belief: pets heal better in the place they feel safest. Everything else grew from there.

Time, not timeslots.

In clinic, appointments are often 10–20 minutes long. Tony saw how that clock shaped conversations — rushing through questions, cutting short explanations, and sometimes leaving owners more anxious than when they arrived. With home visits, he books a full 60 minutes as standard, so no one feels hurried.

Curiosity over assumptions.

Tony's style is quietly curious. He asks about your pet's routines, quirks, and history before jumping to conclusions. He listens first, examines second, and explains everything as he goes — in language you don't need a medical degree to understand.

Honest advice, even when it costs us.

One of Tony's core principles is simple: he tells you what you don't need, not just what you do. No upselling, no unnecessary tests. Trust is built when a vet could charge more and chooses not to.

"Favourite Appointment Energy".

Inside the team, Tony talks about something he calls "Favourite Appointment Energy" — the feeling you get when you're heading to see a pet you already know and love. Every visit should feel like that, for everyone involved.

It's why you'll never feel rushed, talked over, or dismissed. The whole team is trained in "Tony's Way" — the same philosophy, same standards, and the same care for detail.
