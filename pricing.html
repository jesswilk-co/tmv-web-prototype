Frequently asked questions

Click any question to see the answer

What does a typical visit cost? ▾

Every visit has two clear parts: a home visit fee and a treatment fee. For example, an annual dog booster is £99 (home visit) + £55 (vaccination) = £154 total. There are never any hidden charges — the total you see is the total you pay. Add-ons like nail clipping (£25) can be added to any visit without an extra home visit fee.

Why is a home visit more expensive than going to a clinic? ▾

When you factor in travel time, petrol, parking (often £5–10 in central London), taxi fares, and the stress on your pet, a clinic visit costs more than the consultation fee alone. Animals are also experts at masking symptoms — in a stressful clinic environment, they can hide signs your vet needs to see. Our vet comes to you with a fully equipped mobile clinic, spends a full 60 minutes with your pet in their own home, and there's no waiting room. Many of our clients find the total cost — and the experience — compares favourably once you account for the hidden costs of getting there.

I have more than one pet — do I pay a home visit fee for each? ▾

No. You pay one home visit fee per visit, regardless of how many pets are seen. Additional pets on the same visit are charged only the treatment fee (£85 per additional pet). This makes multi-pet households significantly better value.

What does the Pack Membership include? ▾

For £39/month per pet, you get up to 3 home visits per year with full consultation included, an annual booster vaccination, nail clipping and anal gland treatment if clinically needed, and 15% off all other treatments and services.

What happens if I need more than 3 visits in a year? ▾

Additional visits beyond your included 3 are charged at a discounted rate of £156.40 (15% off the standard £184). You'll continue to receive your 15% member discount on all treatment fees too.

I have two pets — is there a multi-pet discount on the membership? ▾

Yes. The first pet is £39/month. Each additional pet in the same household is 20% off — so £31.20/month. Both pets get the full membership benefits including included visits, vaccinations, and discounts.

Can I cancel my membership? ▾

The Pack Membership is a 12-month commitment. You can choose to pay monthly or upfront — paying upfront saves you 10%. If you cancel before the end of your 12 months, you keep your benefits until the end of that billing month. You can always switch back to pay-as-you-go pricing when your commitment ends.

I'm already paying for pet insurance — why do I need a membership? ▾

Pet memberships like ours cover routine costs like vaccinations and check-ups. It helps save on regular expenses, whereas pet insurance covers unexpected, high-cost emergencies and illnesses. Memberships are ideal for helping to budget annual care, while insurance protects against major financial burdens. They work well together — one handles the everyday, the other handles the unexpected.

What area do you cover? ▾

We cover most of Greater London. Enter your postcode on our booking page to check availability in your area. We're expanding our coverage all the time.

How long is each appointment? ▾

Every appointment is 60 minutes as standard. That's 3–4 times longer than a typical 15-minute clinic consultation. It means we can do a thorough health check, deliver any treatments, answer all your questions, and still have time to give your pet some fuss.

What's included in the first visit for new customers? ▾

Your first visit is just £99 — and the consultation fee is waived. It's a full 60-minute appointment including a comprehensive health check, microchip scan, and time to discuss your pet's needs. It's our way of letting you experience Tony's difference before committing. Additional pets are just £50 each on your first visit.

Can you do surgeries or operations at home? ▾

We don't perform surgical procedures like spaying or neutering at home. We focus on what can be done brilliantly in a home setting: consultations, vaccinations, health checks, microchipping, prescriptions, blood tests, and end-of-life care. We also offer sedation, which can make treatment more comfortable, and we can do minor wound treatments like staples or sutures. If your pet needs surgery, we'll refer you to a trusted clinic partner and coordinate the handover.

Do you do Animal Health Certificates for travel? ▾

Yes. We can issue Animal Health Certificates at home — no need to take your pet to a clinic before a stressful journey. The cost is £249 (£99 home visit + £150 certificate). For Pack Members using one of their included visits, the certificate is just £127.50 (15% member discount on £150). Additional pets on the same visit are £120 each (£102 for members).

How does end-of-life care work? ▾

Saying goodbye at home, surrounded by family, is one of the most important things we do. We've created a dedicated End of Life Care page with transparent pricing and information about how we support families through this time. Pack Members receive 15% off all end-of-life services.

How do I know if my puppy or kitten needs a vaccine? ▾

Puppies and kittens need a primary course of vaccinations to build up their immunity. This usually involves two or three injections, given a few weeks apart. If you've just brought your new pet home, check with the breeder, rescue, or previous owner to find out which vaccinations have already been given and which brand was used — it's important to continue with the same brand for the full course.

If your puppy or kitten has already had their first vaccination, we can continue the course at home. If they haven't started yet, we can begin the full course with you. Puppy and kitten first vaccinations are £159 per visit (£99 home visit + £60 vaccination). Once the primary course is complete, your pet will need an annual booster to stay protected.

If you're not sure what your pet has had or what they need next, just get in touch and we'll help you work it out.

Do you offer sedation, and how much does it cost? ▾

Yes, our vets can sedate your pet at home. When and if it's required, we book extra time for the appointment so our vets can monitor pets as they wake up. The price depends on the species and weight of your pet. Our prices range from £85 to £164. Pack Members receive 15% off sedation.
