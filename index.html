When your pet is relaxed at home, we see their true behaviour. We catch signs that disappear in a clinic setting — like a slight limp or a tender spot hidden by anxiety. Home isn't just more comfortable. It's medically better.

The Tony's Difference

Four promises we keep, every visit.

Your pet stays put.
No carriers, no car rides, no waiting rooms. We come to where your pet already feels safe. Less stress means better healing, better diagnosis, and a better experience for everyone.
Time, not timeslots.
Most vet appointments are 10–20 minutes. We book a full 60 minutes as standard, because good care can't be rushed. We take the time to examine thoroughly, explain clearly, and answer every question.
We remember everything.
Every vet reviews your full history before arriving. We know your pet's name, your concerns, what happened last time. Continuity of care isn't a feature – it's how we operate.
Honest advice, always.
We tell you what you don't need, not just what you do. No upselling. No unnecessary tests. Trust is built in the moments where we could charge more and choose not to.
Why a home visit?

Everything is easier when your pet stays home.

No travel — save on petrol, parking, taxis, and time in London traffic.
No waiting room — your appointment starts on time, in your living room.
Less stress for your pet — calmer at home means more accurate examinations.
The whole family can be there — everyone hears the advice first-hand.

Common Questions

Straight answers. No jargon.

What does the £99 first visit include? +
We waive the consultation fee for your very first appointment. We'll do a comprehensive nose-to-tail health check, a microchip scan, and book 60 minutes to discuss your pet's needs. This is only available per household, for a single pet. If you're a multi-pet family, we're happy to look at all the pets in the house during this visit — additional pets will be charged a £50 fee per pet.
How long is each appointment? +
Every appointment is 60 minutes as standard — that's 3–4 times longer than a typical 15-minute clinic consultation. We don't rush.
Will I see the same vet each time? +
Wherever possible, yes. And even when you see a different vet, they'll have reviewed your pet's full history before arriving — so you never have to repeat yourself.
What areas of London do you cover? +
We cover most of Greater London. Enter your postcode on our booking page to check availability.
