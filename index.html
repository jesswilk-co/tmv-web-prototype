The Tony's difference

Why our clients stay with us.

Four simple pillars guide every visit — from the first hello on your doorstep to the follow-up notes after we leave.

Pillar 1

Your pet stays put.

No carriers, no car rides, no waiting rooms. We come to where your pet already feels safe. Less stress means better healing, better diagnosis, and a better experience for everyone.

Pillar 2

Time, not timeslots.

Most vet appointments are 10–20 minutes. We book a full 60 minutes as standard, so we can examine thoroughly, explain clearly, and answer every question. Good care can’t be rushed.

Pillar 3

We remember everything.

Every vet reviews your full history before arriving. We know your pet's name, your concerns, what happened last time. Continuity of care isn’t a feature — it’s how we operate.

Pillar 4

Honest advice. Always.

We tell you what you don't need, not just what you do. No upselling. No unnecessary tests. Trust is built in the moments where we could charge more and choose not to.

Why home visits

Why home visits are worth it.

When your pet stays where they feel safe, everything gets easier — for them, for you, and for the vet.

No travel — save on petrol, parking, taxis, and time in London traffic.
No waiting room — your appointment starts on time, in your living room, not ours.
Less stress for your pet — calmer at home means more accurate examinations and happier visits.
The whole family can be there — everyone hears the advice first-hand and can ask questions.
