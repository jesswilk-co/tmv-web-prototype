What we can do at home.

From routine vaccinations to complex, ongoing conditions, we bring a wide range of veterinary care to your living room — and we’re clear about what still needs a clinic.

Everyday care

Day-to-day vet care, without the waiting room.

Comprehensive wellness exams.

Full nose-to-tail health checks in your pet’s own environment. We take time to observe how they move, rest, and interact at home — details we’d never see in a clinic.

Best for: annual check-ups, new pet health checks, and peace-of-mind visits.

Vaccinations.

Puppy and kitten primary courses, annual boosters (cat, dog, rabbit), rabies, and kennel cough — all delivered at home with clear reminders of what’s due next.

See full prices on our Plans & Pricing page.

Routine illness & ailments.

From ear infections and itchy skin to upset stomachs and stiffness, we diagnose and treat common issues where your pet is calmest.

We’ll always explain what we’re looking for and when we’d be worried.

Microchipping.

Quick, stress-free microchipping at home. A legal requirement for dogs and strongly recommended for cats — especially those who like to wander.

Can be added to any visit without an extra home visit fee.

Parasite control.

Deworming, flea and tick treatment, and preventative plans tailored to your pet’s lifestyle and home environment.

We’ll help you understand what’s truly needed — and what isn’t.

Personalised treatment plans.

For pets with chronic or complex conditions, we build a clear, written plan and keep the same vet involved wherever possible, so nothing gets lost between visits.

Ideal for: long-term conditions, senior pets, and ongoing monitoring.

Travel & healthcare certificates.

Animal Health Certificates issued at home, with all the checks and paperwork done before you travel — no extra trip to the clinic just for forms.

AHC pricing and member discounts are detailed on our Plans & Pricing page.

Pet plans (Pack Membership).

Preventative care bundled into a simple monthly membership — with up to three home visits a year, an annual booster, free add-ons, and 15% off treatments.

Learn more on our Pack Membership section.

End-of-life care.

Gentle, compassionate euthanasia at home, surrounded by the people who love your pet most. We guide you through each step — from sedation to aftercare.

Packages start at £450. Members receive a discount on all end-of-life services.

Insurance claims support.

We help with the paperwork and provide the clinical information your insurer needs, so you can focus on your pet rather than forms.

Available for both Pay As You Go clients and Pack Members.

What we don't do (and why)

Some things still belong in a clinic.

We don’t perform surgical procedures such as spaying, neutering, or complex operations at home. Those belong in a fully equipped surgical theatre, with a full team on hand.

Working with trusted clinic partners.

When your pet needs surgery, we refer you to partners we trust and coordinate the handover — including records, history, and notes from your home visits. That way you’re not repeating yourself, and every clinician knows your pet as more than a file.

Not sure what you need?

If you’re ever unsure whether something can be done at home or needs a clinic, we’ll be honest with you. A quick conversation before you book can save you time, money, and worry.

When in doubt, start with a £99 home visit. We’ll assess your pet calmly at home and make a clear plan together.
