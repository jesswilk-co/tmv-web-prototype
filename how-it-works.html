What to expect on your first visit.

Your first appointment is designed to give you a complete picture of your pet's health — without rushing, pressure, or jargon.

A full hour with your vet.

We book 60 minutes as standard — 3–4 times longer than a typical clinic appointment. That gives us time to examine your pet thoroughly and answer every question.

Nose-to-tail health check.

From eyes and ears to joints and heart, we check each part of your pet carefully and explain what we're looking for in plain language.

Microchip scan and history review.

We scan your pet's microchip, review any records you have, and build a clear, up-to-date picture of their health and routine.

What we can do at home

What we can — and can't — do at home.

We focus on everything that can be done brilliantly in a home setting. When your pet needs surgery, we work with trusted clinic partners instead.

What we can do.

Consultations for new concerns and ongoing conditions. Vaccinations, boosters, microchipping, and parasite control. Health checks, blood tests, and prescription reviews. End-of-life care at home, with full support for your family.

What we don't do (and why).

We don't perform surgical procedures such as spaying or neutering at home. These are best carried out in a controlled clinic environment. When surgery is needed, we refer you to trusted partners and coordinate the handover — no gaps, no repeating yourself.
